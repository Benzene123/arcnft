ARC ORIGIN

How it all began . . .

Prologue

In an uknown solar system, a race of beings known only as The "Ancients", began to explore worlds outside their own homes in hopes of discovering a new home. Their planet was on the brink of destruction due to overutilization, making it increasenly unhabitable. With their scientists desperate for a solution, they began to conceive extreme methods of terraforming candidate planets into replacement. The fruit of this effort was a strange dust-like chemical that could be used to produce the unique gas necessary to make an environment livable for their race.

Three district planets within their local solar system were chosen to receive the gas. After extensive research, it was estimated that the conversion process would take a few hundred years. With this new information they decided to preserve themselves cyrogenically. A few Ancients were selected and tasked with keeping watch with the help of a few droids.

Unbeknowest to them, there was one scientist who believed robbing life of other worlds to save their own was unjust. He immediately began to formulate a plan to undermine his fellow Ancients. Believing that the gas would kill off the infected wildlife, he created his own variation that would instead trigger a mutation that would immunity to the disease born out of the gas. He created his own variation that would instead trigger a mutation that would provide immunity to the disease born of the gas. He then developed transmission devices, in the shape of idols, hoping that his variant would give selected beings a chance at defending themselves against The Ancients. He inscribed a message on each idol and launched a pod to the three different planets

Following the path of the first pod, he sent a customized droid. Programmed in its mind was one plan. "Stop The Ancients".
